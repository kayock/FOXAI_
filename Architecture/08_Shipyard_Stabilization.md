# Shipyard Stabilization

The Shipyard is the quality gate for FOXAI.

Installed tools:

- Ruff
- Black
- MyPy
- PyDeps
- Import-Linter
- Grimp
- Pip-Audit
- CycloneDX BOM

## Shipyard Stations

### Dry Dock
Formatting and linting.

### Structural Analysis
Import graph and architecture rules.

### Security Inspection
Dependency audit and SBOM.

### Flight Readiness
Dependency Arbiter, service checks, FOXKernel boot.

## Commissioning Requirement

No new department is commissioned until Shipyard passes.
