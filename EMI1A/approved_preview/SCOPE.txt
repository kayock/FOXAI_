Extension Manager Phase 1 objective

Create a trustworthy inventory and health dashboard without duplicating the
existing Extension Manager shell.

Required:
- detect applications, departments, engines, models, projectors, and optional
  tools;
- show VERIFIED / INSTALLED / MISSING / NEEDS ATTENTION;
- show exact path, version, size, source, and health basis;
- distinguish required from optional;
- filter projectors out of language-model listings;
- provide safe operator-initiated Open Folder and allowlisted Launch controls;
- remain read-only during inventory refresh;
- do not create extension_state.json when it is absent;
- preserve existing manifest controls unchanged.

Not included:
- installation;
- removal;
- updating;
- registry or manifest rewriting;
- automatic enabling/disabling.
