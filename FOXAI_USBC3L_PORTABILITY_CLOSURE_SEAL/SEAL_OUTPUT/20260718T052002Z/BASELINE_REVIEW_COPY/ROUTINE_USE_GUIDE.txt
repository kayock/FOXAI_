FOXAI COMFYUI — ROUTINE USE

Known-good baseline: ComfyUI_USB_C3_KNOWN_GOOD_20260718T045851Z

START
  Double-click START_COMFYUI_NORMAL.bat
  The browser opens only after the local service passes its health check.

STATUS
  Double-click STATUS_COMFYUI_NORMAL.bat

STOP
  Double-click STOP_COMFYUI_NORMAL.bat
  It stops only the exact controller-owned ComfyUI process.

VERIFY AFTER MOVING THE USB OR WHEN SOMETHING SEEMS WRONG
  Double-click VERIFY_COMFYUI_KNOWN_GOOD.bat
  This performs a full offline verification of all 39,046 dependency files.

NORMAL SAFETY PROFILE
  CPU only; custom nodes disabled; localhost 127.0.0.1:8188 only.
  No automatic log deletion and no automatic force-kill.

LOGS
  Runtime\ComfyUI\logs\normal\<run-id>
  A warning is planned after 100 runs or 1 GiB, but cleanup is never automatic.
