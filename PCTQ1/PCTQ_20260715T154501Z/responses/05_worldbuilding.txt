1. **Council of Bloom** – Decisions are made through a ritual where each citizen plants a seed in a communal soil bed, and growth patterns dictate the path forward.  
2. **Fermented Lichen Porridge** – A staple meal made by soaking and fermenting the machine’s own lichen, aged for months to develop complex flavors.  
3. **Rite of Luminous Steps** – Children are taught to walk backward for their first hundred steps, symbolizing humility and learning to see the world from others’ perspectives.  
4. **Weeping Stone** – Mourning involves whispering secrets into a smooth stone, which is then placed in a slow-moving river to carry memories away.  
5. **Chromatic Weaving** – Artists thread bioluminescent algae into tapestries that shift color with the passage of time, telling stories through subtle hue changes.  
6. **Guiding Roots** – Navigation relies on interpreting the growth patterns of lichen on the machines, which respond to the collective emotional state of the city.  
7. **Taboo of Rushing** – It is forbidden to move faster than the city’s pace, as doing so disrupts the harmony between the people and the machines.
