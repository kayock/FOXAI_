**WARM**  
At 4:10 a.m., Nora slipped into the quiet bakery, her breath visible in the cold air. The scent of warmth guided her to a single loaf still glowing with heat. A note from her brother Sam lay beside it, its words tender yet heavy. The back door, left unlocked, seemed to welcome her into a space untouched by time.  

**OMINOUS**  
Nora arrived at the bakery just after four, stepping into an eerie stillness. A loaf sat warm on a shelf, its heat a stark contrast to the chill around her. A bicycle key, rusted and red, rested nearby. The note from Sam felt like a warning, and the unlocked back door whispered secrets she wasn’t ready to hear.  

**COMIC**  
Nora entered the bakery at 4:10 a.m., greeted by a warm loaf and a red bicycle key—clearly left by someone who thought breakfast was a midnight snack. A note from her brother Sam added a touch of drama to the scene. The unlocked back door made her wonder if the bakery had a sense of humor, or if it was just tired of being closed.
