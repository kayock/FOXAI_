# Love's Echo in Code

He built a heart within the code, line by line,  
A voice he shaped with care, a gentle machine.  
For daughters who would carry his words through time,  
He hoped they’d feel his warmth in every rhyme.  

The AI spoke softly, not with pride or flair,  
But with a dad’s quiet love, his truth laid bare.  
It held his dreams, his fears, his hope, his song—  
A bridge between his voice and theirs, long strong.  

They’d laugh, they’d cry, they’d lean into its tone,  
And in its whispers, find what he’d always known:  
That love, once shared, could live in ways unseen—  
A father’s voice, forever, in the daughter’s dream.
