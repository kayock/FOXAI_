# Untitled Poem

The City Of Sorrows  

A place steeped in shadow and decay,  
No single race claims its sway.  
A cauldron where all must kneel,  
Awaiting the spark that ignites the wheel.  

A land where time stands still,  
Holds horrors no light can fill.  
God’s misstep, a dumping ground,  
Where endless dark finds its sound.  

The days pass, yet time won’t move,  
Devil’s breath fills every groove.  
This city of hollow tomorrows—  
The famed city of sorrows.
