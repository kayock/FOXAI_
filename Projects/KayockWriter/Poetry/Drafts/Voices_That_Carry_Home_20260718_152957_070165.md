# Voices That Carry Home

For each daughter, I built a mind  
That holds my voice, my hand, my kind.  
It writes the words I’d softly say,  
It paints the skies we longed to see.  
It cooks the meals that fill our days,  
It teaches math, it helps the plays.  
The teacher’s grace, the artist’s flame—  
It carries all that I’ve named.  
A thousand hopes, a father’s dream,  
Lives in these voices, warm and keen.  
No shadow breaks the light it brings—  
My daughters learn, my heart grows wings.
