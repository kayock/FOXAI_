# Whispers in the Storm

Frodo’s hand trembles as the sky cracks open,  
Legolas draws his bow, though he knows the cost.  
The earth groans under Morgoth’s heavy reign,  
And Sauron, broken, lies in his master’s stain.  

The wind howls secrets of a coming war,  
Their paths entwined, yet fate feels too far.  
No ring can shield what love alone might bear—  
They stand, two hearts, against the endless air.  

Legolas speaks, though fear grips his throat,  
“Let us face what shadows have sown.”  
Frodo nods, though tears blur his sight,  
And together they meet the dark’s might.  

The world may fall, but they’ll fight to stay—  
For hope, for home, for the light of day.
